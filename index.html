About Me

Tiffany Bell

I am a Registered Early Childhood Educator Travelling the World

Tiffany is a Registered Early Childhood Educator from Ontario, Canada. She has combined her passion for childhood education and travelling. In her thirteen year career she has visited countries such as the United States of America, South Korea, Japan, Vietnam and New Zealand.

She currently resides in Melboourne , Australia by the beach where she hones her skills as a coder while rustling three lovely, energetic children to primary , secondary and Pre school.

When Tiffany isn't coding or working with children and their families. She is at the beach reading a book, exploring Farmer's Markets and other street food venodr or playing PC games. Sims anyone?
